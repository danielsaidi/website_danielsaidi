--- title: Hybrid Apps date: 2014-12-01 location: CocoaHeads Sthlm location_url: https://www.meetup.com/CocoaHeads-Stockholm/ abstract: | In this talk, we take a look at how Apple's vision of 3rd party iOS apps went from mobile web browser experiences to the native apps we have today. We then go through a case study on how a Swedish high-profile company replaced their native apps with custom-made, hybrid apps that wrapped their new responsive web site. ---
