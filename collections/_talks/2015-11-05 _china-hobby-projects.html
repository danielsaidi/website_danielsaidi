--- title: Hobby Projects Always! date: 2015-11-05 location: Rebla, Stockholm location_url: https://rebla.se redirect_to: /talks/custom/2015-11-05_china-hobby-projects abstract: | I gave this talk to a Chinese group of college board representatives. I talked about how hobby projects can bring happiness, friendship and creativity and how amazing I think it is to create things together with others, be it music, fanzines or apps. I then talked about some collaborative projects, where my family worked with family members in San Francisco, that we had never met before, to make apps for children. ---
